Peach Marmalade

Ingredients

1 - 1 1/2 kilos of peaches

2 - 1 kilo of sugar

3 - 1 cup water

3 - 1 cinnamon stick

Procedure

1 -Peel the peaches, remove the pits, cut them into quarters and place them in a bowl in layers, alternating with the sugar.

2 - Then let them macerate for 1 hour and cook over high heat for 10 minutes. Add water and cinnamon.

3 - Then continue cooking over a low heat, stirring with a wooden spoon when it begins to thicken.

4 - When it thickens, leave in the fire for 10 more minutes and remove from the fire. Let cool and serve.
