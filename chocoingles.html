Chocolate Gelatine

Chocolate gelatine

Ingredients

8 Servings

A cup of sugar

2 litres of milk

1 - tablespoon vanilla

2 - cinnamon slice

sachets of blackretina powder 7g each, hydrated

1/2 cup traditional table chocolate powder

1/4 cup condensed milk

1/4 cup chocolate syrup

Procedure

1 - Heat the milk with the sugar in a small pot over medium heat. Add vanilla essence and cinnamon, cook for 10 minutes or until ingredients release their aroma.

2 - Add the previously hydrated grenetine and move so that it dissolves with the heat of the preparation.

3 - Separate the preparation into 3 different bowls. To the first add q tza of table chocolate, to the second ½ cup and to the third the condensed milk.

4 - Pour the condensed milk mixture into a gelatin mold and refrigerate until curdled. Repeat with the other two preparations, making sure that the layers of colours are well defined.

5 - Remove from refrigeration, demould the gelatin and cut a portion, decorate with chocolate syrup.
