Strawberry gelatine

1 - 4 cups hot water

2 - 1 packet strawberry gelatin

3 - 2 sachets of hydrated and melted grenetine

4 - 1 1/2 cups of strawberries cut to size from the mold, washed and disinfected

5 - 2 cups condensed milk

6 - 2 cups of milk

7 - 1 cup evaporated milk

8 - 6 sachets of hydrated and melted grenetine

enough strawberry to decorate

enough mint to decorate

Procedure

1 - Dissolve the strawberry gelatine sachet in the hot water and add the grenetine. Mix well.

2 - Pour a spoon into a 10 cm diameter round mold, refrigerate until it sets.

3 - Place the strawberries on top of the curdled gelatine and pour in the rest of the strawberry gelatine, refrigerate until curdled. Remove the molds and set aside.

4 - Heat the milk with the condensed milk, the evaporated milk, add the grenetine and mix very well to avoid lumps. Cool at room temperature taking care not to curdle.

5 - Pour 2 tablespoons of milk gelatine over a round mould 20 cm in diameter, refrigerate until it sets.

6 - Place the strawberry jelly on top of the milk jelly and pour the rest of the jelly until covered. Refrigerate until it sets completely.

7 - Demould and decorate with strawberries and mint.

Recipe Note

Strawberry is a very acid fruit, so it is necessary to add a little more grenetine, this will give greater strength to the gelatine and avoid the risk of not setting..
