Homemade flan


Ingredients

1 - 200 grams of condensed milk

2 - 4 egg pieces

3 - 1 teaspoon vanilla

4 - 200 grams of fresh milk

5 - 4 tablespoons sugar

Procedure

Important note

The mixture of the elements has to be used as a mixer.

1 - Preheat oven to 350 degrees

2 - Mix the eggs, condensed milk, fresh milk and vanilla in a bowl and beat until well mixed..

3 - Melt the sugar until it reaches the point of caramel in a saucepan. Put the caramel melted in the mold and left to coolv.

4 - Once the caramel has hardened, add the mixture of eggs, milk, etc.

5 - Put a tray with water and place the mold on top and let it cook in the oven for 30 minutes in the bain-marie..

6 - Leave to cool and store in the refrigerator.

Note Extra

What's the bain-marie? To heat a bain-marie you need to put a small container inside a larger one containing water.
