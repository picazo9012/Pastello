Hot Chocolate Drinks

Nutella chocolate

Ingredients

2 cups milk for nutella chocolate

1 cup whipping cream for nutella chocolate

1/4 cup nutella

1/2 cup melted bitter chocolate

1/2 cup nutella To frost cup

Recipe Note

The decoration is optional and the way you prefer

Procedure

1 - For nutella chocolate: Heats milk, whipping cream, Nutella and chocolate. Cook over low heat for 10 minutes or until slightly thickened.

2 - Frost a cup with Nutella and walnuts. Serve hot chocolate and decorate with whipped cream, walnuts and melted chocolate.
