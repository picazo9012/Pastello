Rompope chocolate

Ingredients

1/2 cup Rompope for Rompope chocolate

1 1/2 cups milk

1/2 cup whipping cream

1 teaspoon vanilla

1/2 cup small pieces of white chocolate

1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

Procedure

1 - For the breaker chocolate, heat in a medium saucepan the breaker with milk, whipping cream, vanilla extract, cinnamon, nutmeg and white chocolate. Cook for 10 minutes or until thickened.

2 - Remove from heat and serve hot in a glass cup

3 - The decoration is optional and to your liking...that you enjoy it.
