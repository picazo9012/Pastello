Hot Chocolate Drinks

Chocolate cookies and cream

Ingredients

2 cups of milk

1 cup evaporated milk

1 taza de galleta de chocolate

1/4 cup white chocolate

cup chocolate chip cookie, ground

2 tablespoons cream

to the taste of chocolate biscuit

Procedure

1 - For cookies and cream chocolate, blend milk with evaporated milk and cookies.

2 - Heat over low heat under previous preparation and add white chocolate, cook for 10 minutes.

3 - Serve the chocolate, decorate with whipped cream and crackers..
