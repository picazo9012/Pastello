Polvorones of Orange

Polvorones of orange

Ingredients

1 - 4 cups flour

2 - 2 cups vegetable shortening

3 - 2 egg pieces

4 - 2 1/4 cups sugar

5 - 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder

6 - 1 teaspoon vanilla

7 - 1 piece orange zest

Procedure

1 - Preheat oven to 175°C.

2 - Put all the ingredients in a bowl, with the help of a spatula place the cream until it mixes perfectly.

3 - On a floured surface, knead the mixture for 5 minutes or until it comes off your hands.

4 - Form small balls of 80 grams and place on a tray, press gently with your hand and bake for 15 minutes. Let cool and reserve.

5 - Accompany with a cup of coffee.
