Three milks dulce de leche cake

Three milks cake


Ingredients

1 - 2 1/8 bars of butter

2 - 3/4 cup sugar

2 - 1/2 cup condensed milk

4 - 6 egg pieces

5 - 3/4 cup flour

6 - 1 teaspoon baking powder

7 - 2 cups condensed milk for the three milks

8 - 2 cups evaporated milk for the three milks

9 - 2 cups whole milk for the three milks

10 - 4 cups dulce de leche

Procedure

1 - Preheat oven to 180° C

2 - Add the butter and sugar to the mixer until it is perfectly cooked, add the condensed milk and the egg one by one, gradually add the flour with the sifter powder. Add until a homogeneous mixture is obtained.

3 - Add the mixture to a greased and floured pan.

4 - Bake 30 minutes until you get a fluffy cake. Place the bread on a grill, cool the cake. Reserve

5 - In a bowl add the condensed milk, evaporated milk, whole milk, mix and add to the bread so that it is hydrated with the mixture of 3 milks. Match the cake, bathe with the 3 milks and refrigerate. Add the rest of the milk mixture.

6 - Softens the dulce de leche and covers the cake taking care not to break it. With a rasp of small teeth decorate forming the lines in all the surface. Keep refrigerated.
