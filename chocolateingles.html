Chocolate Cupcake

Chocolate cupcake


Ingredints

1 - 250 grams of wheat flourv

2 - 125 grams of brown sugar (or any other, according to personal taste)

3 - 3/5 cup milk

4 - 125 grams of butter

5 - 25 grams of cocoa

6 - 1 egg

7 - 1 teaspoon yeast

Procedure

Important data

Everything has to be a mixer because the mixture must have air to inflate the cupcake, you can also enrebolver by hand but in a special way, even though it's later.

1 - Mix the butter, the egg and the sugar (The butter must not be cold).

2 - In another bowl we must mix the cocoa, flour and yeast.

3 - Presenting the oven at 180 degrees depends on the oven if it is celsius or fahrenheit, you need to look for equivalence (Do not leave the mixture outside, the air comes out and the mixture will go down and not sponge).

4 - Then on top of the matequilla mixture, sift a little of the mixture of flour and cocoa and add a little milk. and then whip (Successively until the milk and the mixture of flour and cocoa are finished)

Note from step 4

The point of pouring little by little is that the flour does not have lumps

4 - Place the portions in the moulds

5 - Bake in the oven for 19 minutes

Recipe Note

The decoration is to personal taste, you can include whipped cream, sparks or even fruits.
