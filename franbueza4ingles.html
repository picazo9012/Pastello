Raspberry Marmalade

Ingredients

1 - 500g granilla sugar

2 - 500g raspberries

Procedure

1 - To make homemade raspberry jam, the first thing you should do is place the previously washed raspberries in a pot along with the sugar.

2 - Take to the fire and stir with a spatula so that the sugar is melting and the raspberry begins to release its juice. It is essential to scrape the sides of the pot so that the sugar does not stick.

3 - Let the raspberry marmalade cook and when it boils, count about 20 minutes, stirring from time to time..

4 - After 20 minutes of cooking the homemade raspberry jam, remove from heat and refrigerate for a space of three hours before use..

As you can see the homemade raspberry jam is very easy to make and has only two ingredients.
