Cheese pie

Ingredients

1 - 5 eggs

2 - 1 can condensed milk

3 - 1 can evaporated milk

4 - 250 grams of cream cheese or double cream

5 - 1 packet vanilla cookie

6 - 1 stick of melted butter (90 gr)

7 - 1 dash of vanilla

8 - 30 grams of butter (to grease the mold)

Procedure

1 - Preheat the oven to 180ºC. Grease a pie pan with the butter..

2 - Mix cheese, milks, egg and vanilla until smooth. This can also be done in a slow blender.

3 - Grind the package of biscuits until powdered and mix with the melted butter to form a dough. Once the dough is ready, cover the mold with it, leaving no empty spaces. The amount of dough depends on each person's taste.

4 - Add the cheese mixture to the mold and place in the oven. In 40 minutes introduce a toothpick, if it comes out clean, remove from the oven and let cool..
