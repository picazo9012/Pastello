Lemon dessert

Dessert of lemon with Maria to refrigerator cookies

Ingredients

2 tubes of Marias cookies

1 can evaporated milk

6 PCs of lemons

Precedure

1 - Part the lemons in half and remove seeds

2 - extract the juice of the lemons

3 - blend evaporated milk, sweetened condensed milk and lemon juice until a cream

4 - In a bowl, the cookies are placed on the base and the previous mixture is added and layers of cookies and the mixture are continued until they reach the top of the container.

5 - At the end to decorate it is recommended to put some pieces of cookies or lemon slices

6 - It is refrigerated for 2 hours and served.
