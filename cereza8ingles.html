Cherry Marmalade

Ingredients

1 - 600 g cherries (clean without stone or corner)

2 - 250 g White sugar

3 - 1/2 lemon (Your juice))

Procedure

1 - We wash and remove the stone and the corner of the cherries.

2 - Place the cherries in a saucepan and cover with sugar and lemon juice..

3 - Mix well and leave to rest in the fridge for a couple of hours.

4 - We take out, we put on the fire (vitro or gas) to the maximum.

5 - When it starts to boil, lower the heat to the minimum and leave to cook for 20 minutes. approximately, mixing from time to time.

6 - Remove from the fire, grind with the mixer (very careful not to splash and burn us), and let it cool..

7 - We taste our homemade cherry jam when and how we like it best.

Recipe Note

Cooking time 30 minutes
