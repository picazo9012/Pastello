Pineapple Marmalade

1 - 2 cups finely chopped pineapple

2 - 4 teaspoons lemon juice

3 - 2 cups sugar

4 -1 cup water

Procedure

1 - Cook the pineapple with the water in a saucepan over low heat for 20 minutes.

2 - Uncover, add lemon juice and sugar.

3 - Cook 40 minutes over low heat or until thickened. Reserve in airtight containers.
