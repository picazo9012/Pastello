Strawberry Marmalade

Ingredients

1 - 2 cups medium strawberries

2 - 3/4 cup sugar

3 - 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Procedure

1 -Wash and disinfects strawberries very well

2 - Cut them into small pieces (quarters)

3 - Place all ingredients in a casserole, heat over low heat so it does not burn.

4 - Bring to a boil and continue stirring until all the sugar is dissolved, hold for 30 minutes.

5 -To find out if this list should be slightly translucent

6 - Turn off the heat and continue moving for 10 minutes until cooled and once ready, store in a glass jar.
