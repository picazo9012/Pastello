Hot Chocolate Drinks

Cajeta chocolate

Ingredients

2 cups of milk for the cajeta chocolate

1/4 cup small chopped chocolate table top

1/2 cup of cajeta

2 tablespoons whipped cream to decorate the cajeta chocolate

to the taste of cajeta to decorate the chocolate of cajeta

Procedure

1 - For the cajeta chocolate, heat the milk with the table chocolate and the cajeta in a small pot.

2 - Cook 10 minutes or until thickened. Serve in a glass cup and decorate with whipped cream and cajeta.

3 - The decoration is to personal taste
