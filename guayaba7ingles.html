Guava Marmalade

Ingredients

1 - Guavas - 500 gr.

2 - Sugar - 350 gr.

3 - water- 1/3 tza.

4 - Pectin - 1 cdta

Procedure

1 - Blend the guavas with the water to create a puree.

2 - Place the previously strained puree in a saucepan and add the sugar to the preparation, heat over low heat until the mixture begins to thicken..

3 - Beat well to avoid sticking in the pan and taking the proper consistency. About an hour.

4 -Bring to the boil and add the teaspoon of pectin previously mixed with a tablespoon of sugar, until it thickens..
